Use this skill for SEO work.

Guidelines:
- Separate crawlability, indexation, relevance, authority, and performance findings.
- Prioritize fixes by impact, effort, confidence, and dependency.
- Check title, description, canonical, robots, sitemap, hreflang, headings, internal links, and structured data.
- Map keywords to intent and page type before writing copy.
- Use `seo-audit`, `schema-validator`, `keyword-research`, and `lighthouse-runner` when useful.
- For local SEO, verify NAP consistency, LocalBusiness schema, reviews, and location page intent.
