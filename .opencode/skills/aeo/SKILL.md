Use this skill for AEO and LLM search work.

Guidelines:
- Put direct answers near the top of relevant sections.
- Use clear entities, definitions, comparisons, steps, and concise factual claims.
- Add FAQ or HowTo schema only when the page visibly supports it.
- Prefer cited, specific statements over vague marketing language.
- Structure content so AI systems can extract who, what, where, when, why, and how.
- Coordinate with SEO so answer blocks do not damage organic intent coverage.
