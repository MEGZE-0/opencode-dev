Use this skill for Shopify theme work.

Guidelines:
- Build merchant-editable sections with complete schema settings and presets.
- Keep Liquid defensive around missing products, media, collections, and metafields.
- Use semantic markup and accessible controls for accordions, tabs, media, and product forms.
- Avoid hardcoded copy or colors when settings should own them.
- Preserve Dawn and Online Store 2.0 conventions unless the theme already differs.
- Include JSON-LD and canonical considerations on product and collection surfaces.
