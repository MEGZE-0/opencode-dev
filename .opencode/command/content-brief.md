Execute this content brief SOP.

1. Run `keyword-research` on the target keyword or topic.
2. Analyze top 5 ranking competitor pages for structure, headings, word count, schema, media, and content gaps.
3. Produce `content-brief.md` with search intent, primary keyword, secondary keywords, LSI terms, title tag under 60 characters, meta description under 155 characters, H1, H2/H3 outline, word count range, schema recommendation, internal links, competitor gaps, and E-E-A-T signals.
4. Ask `aeo-specialist` to add direct-answer, FAQ, and AI citation guidance.
5. Ask `seo-specialist` to review technical fit and internal linking.
6. Pass the final brief to `content-seo-writer` for a full draft only when requested.
