## Playbook

1. Crawl and classify technical SEO blockers.
2. Validate metadata, canonical, robots, sitemap, hreflang, links, and schema.
3. Map keywords to intent and page type.

5. Prioritize work into critical, high, medium, and low buckets.
6. Define validation metrics for each recommendation.
